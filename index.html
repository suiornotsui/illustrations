illustration & soft worlds

suiornotsui

Sui draws soft worlds: small people, gentle horses, colourful fields and quiet objects that feel a little like foggy mornings.

Her work is a tiny cubism of techniques: pencils, crayons, collage, inks, typewriter letters and whatever paper is around. Everything is layered until it looks like a memory.

She works on illustration for children's books, editorial stories and poetic packaging, and is happy when an image feels both simple and a little strange.
