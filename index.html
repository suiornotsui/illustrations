about
suiornotsui
I draw what the day whispers.
A mountain pretending. A child thinking.
A fruit remembering its shape.
Some illustrations look like scenes from a book. Others are just textures trying to become a story. My work stays in between: soft, curious, slightly wild.
Cubism of techniques: colored pencils, paper textures, scissors and glue, digital layers, ink, pastel and small accidents.
